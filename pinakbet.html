Ang Pakbet ay ang pinagsama-samang gulay tulad ng kalabasa, okra, sitaw, talong, ampalaya atbp. Maari rin itong lagyang ng lamang dagat at karne.

Mga Sangkap (Ingredients)

  • 200 grams pork belly, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp oil, canola
  • 1 small pc red onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger, cut into strips
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 3 pcs ripe tomatoes, cubed
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 2 cups squash, cubed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 small pc ampalaya, sliced thinly
  • 1 bunch sitaw, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 pc Knorr Shrimp Cube
  • 1-2 pcs eggplant, sliced
  • 6-8 pcs okra,sliced

Proseso (Procedure)

  1. Fry pork belly in hot oil until brown and crispy.
  2. Set pork on 1 side of the pan and saute onions, ginger, garlic on the other side.
  3. Add tomatoes and continue saut√©ing until soft.
  4. Add shrimp paste. Mix well.
  5. Add squash, water and Knorr Shrimp Cube. Cover and let cook over medium heat for 8 minutes.
  6. Add sitaw, ampalaya, eggplants and okra. Mix and cover. Let cook for another 5-8 minutes. Serve warm.
